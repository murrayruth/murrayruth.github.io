Marine Protected Areas

Many countries signed agreements to protect 10% of coastal and marine habitats by 2020. Over 15,000 marine protected areas now exist, but there are increasing concerns over their effectivenss. For MPAs to be of value for biodiversity conservation and to reach these targets, they must be effective and equitable with the sharing of benefits to promote sustainable development.
